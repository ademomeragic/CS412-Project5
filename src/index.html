Chill with the nature.

Modern place to relax.

The campsite Veliki Čair Bjelašnica is conceived as a tourist resort located on the mountain Bjelašnica in a quiet and secluded area part of the mountain in abundance of forests and glades, and near the main facilities of the Olympic Center. Hotels, apartment resort, ski slopes, Babin Do are located about 2km from the camp, and the connection is provided with a wide asphalt road. Javorov Do, which is known as a regular picnic area for people of Sarajevo is also away from the camp at just 2.5 km and is connected to the promenade and macadam forest road.

Easy access from the main road, which is 150m away from the camp, makes the location even more attractive, and on the location is completely quiet and peaceful on the basis of the Wide Forest Belt. Infrastructure (electricity, gas and water) is provided on the site, and thus implementation is enabled for the imagined project.
